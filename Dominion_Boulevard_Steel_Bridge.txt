Dominion Boulevard Steel Bridge (known locally as simply the Steel Bridge) is
 a double leaf bascule, two lane drawbridge which spans the southern branch of
 the Elizabeth River in the City of Chesapeake in South Hampton Roads in
 southeastern Virginia. It carries U.S. Route 17 in Virginia which is Dominion
 Boulevard (formerly numbered Virginia Route 104). The bridge was built in 1962
 and is operated by the City of Chesapeake.
